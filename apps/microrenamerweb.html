MicroRenamerWEB

Web companion to the desktop renamer — PWA layout, alt-text generation service, and page components. Embedded inside MicroRenamerWPF's "PWA Resize Tool" tab via WebView2.

Blazor Server.NET 9 RadzenWebView2/PWA
